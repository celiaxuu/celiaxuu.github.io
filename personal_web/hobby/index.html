Learn About My Hobbies!

Music

I have been playing the flute for over 15 years and the violin for over 8 years. Throughout this journey, I naturally became a fan of classical music. For me, what matters more than the performance itself is the patience and perseverance cultivated during the process of learning and practicing, as well as the comfort and inspiration that music provides me, no matter when or where.

Reading

Reading always brings me calm and inspiration. My favorite book is "The Myth of Sisyphus" by Camus. Recently, I've developed a strong interest in Latin American literature. However, traditional Chinese literature is the most important to me. I also enjoy participating in book clubs, as hearing others' different thoughts on the same work provides new perspectives and ideas.

Volleyball

Volleyball is a sport that I really enjoy, not just because it helps me relieve stress, but also because it offers a fantastic opportunity to connect with others. As a team sport, it fosters collaboration and camaraderie, making it a great way to meet new people and strengthen friendships. The shared experiences and mutual support on the court create a strong sense of community that goes beyond the game itself.
